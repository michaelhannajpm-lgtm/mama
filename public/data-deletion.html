Your data, your choice

Delete your data

Last updated: June 11, 2026

You can delete your Go Mama account and all of the personal data associated with it at any time. Here's how. We never sell or share your personal data — and when you ask us to delete it, we do.

How to request deletion

  1. Email privacy@gomama.app with the subject "Delete my data".
  2. Send it from the email address on your account — or mention the phone number you signed up with — so we can verify the request is really you.
  3. We'll confirm, permanently delete your account and personal data, and email you when it's done.

That's the only verification we need — there's nothing else you have to fill out.

Deleting data from Facebook login

If you signed in with Facebook, you can also remove Go Mama's access from your Facebook App Settings. To delete the data we received through Facebook, use either option above and we'll erase it.

What gets deleted

Your account, profile details, kids' info, photos, messages, matches, saved items, availability, and any data we obtained from a connected Facebook or Instagram account. We remove this from our systems within a reasonable period. We may retain a minimal record only where the law requires it (for example, to comply with legal obligations or resolve a dispute), as described in our Privacy Policy.

How long it takes

In-app deletions take effect immediately — your profile is hidden and your access is removed at once. We then fully erase your data within 30 days. If you change your mind during that window, simply log back in to restore your account. Email requests are completed promptly, and in any case within 30 days. Need help? Email privacy@gomama.app.
