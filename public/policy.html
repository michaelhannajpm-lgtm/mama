Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Go Mama helps moms find real friendship with other moms nearby. Your trust is the whole point — so here's the short version: we collect only what we need to run the service, we never sell or rent your personal data, and we don't use it for advertising. The details below explain exactly what we collect and why.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how Go Mama ("Go Mama", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile and web application and related services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — your first name, and a phone number or email address used to create and secure your account.
  • Profile information — things you choose to add, such as your kids' ages, interests, the type of mom community you're looking for, your general neighborhood or area, a short bio, and photos.
  • Availability & preferences — the days, times, and places you're open to meeting, used to suggest matches.
  • Verification signals — to keep the community safe and "verified-only", we may ask you to connect a social account (such as Facebook or Instagram) and add a real photo.
  • Messages & activity — content you send to other moms in the app and actions you take (matches, saved items, RSVPs).

Information from Facebook & Instagram login

If you choose to sign in with or connect Facebook or Instagram, we receive a limited set of information from those platforms based on the permissions you grant — typically your name, profile photo, and email address. We use this only to create your account, verify that you're a real person, and personalize your experience. We do not post to your social accounts, and we do not access your friends list, posts, or contacts. You can revoke this connection at any time in your Facebook or Instagram settings, or by contacting us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data — basic technical information such as device type, browser, app version, and how you interact with features, used to keep the Service working and improve it.
  • Approximate location — derived from the area you tell us or, with your permission, your device, so we can show moms and places near you. You can use the app with only a general area instead of precise location.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and verify you're a real mom.
  • To match you with nearby moms, groups, places, and events that fit your preferences.
  • To enable messaging and meetups between you and your matches.
  • To keep the community safe, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms.
  • To send you account, safety, and service-related notifications (you control optional notifications).
  • To understand, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.

Our promise about your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with data brokers, and we do not use it to serve third-party advertising or to build advertising profiles. We do not sell your messages or use the personal content you share to train AI models. We use your data only to run Go Mama and keep it safe.

4. How information is shared

We share information only in these limited ways:

  • With other moms — the profile details you choose to make visible (such as your first name, photo, general area, and shared interests) are shown to potential matches so you can connect. You control what goes on your profile, and free vs. full profile visibility is limited by design. We never reveal your exact contact information (phone/email) to other users.
  • With service providers — trusted vendors that operate the Service on our behalf, such as our authentication and database provider (Supabase) and our hosting provider (Vercel). They process data only to provide their service to us and are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
  • For legal & safety reasons — if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, the public, or Go Mama.
  • In a business transfer — if Go Mama is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may transfer as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we must retain limited records to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

6. Your rights & how to delete your data

You can access and update most of your information directly in the app's Profile screen. You may also request to access, correct, or permanently delete your personal data at any time.

  • In the app — edit your profile, photos, contact details, and preferences from the Profile tab.
  • Delete your account & data — email us at privacy@gomama.app with the subject "Delete my data", from the email or referencing the phone number on your account. We will verify your request and delete your personal information.
  • Facebook data — to disconnect Facebook, remove Go Mama from your Facebook App Settings. To delete the data we obtained through Facebook, use the email request above.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA (for example, to object to or restrict processing, or to data portability). We honor these rights — contact us to exercise them, and we will not discriminate against you for doing so.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards — including encryption in transit, access controls, and a least-privilege data model — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is ever 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your data and to limit who can access it.

8. Children's privacy

Go Mama is built for adults — parents and caregivers who are 18 or older. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has given us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.

10. Contact us

Questions, requests, or concerns about your privacy? Email us at privacy@gomama.app and we'll be glad to help.
